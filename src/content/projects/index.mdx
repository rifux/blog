This registry is dedicated to creations in active development, projects already set loose, or systems whose architectural designs and inner workings are worth documenting. Each entry acts as a brief scroll: demystifying why the project exists, the problem it tames, its current spellbook of capabilities, and its path of future evolution.

Documentation is kept deliberately lightweight, avoiding the temptation to force information into heavy display cards. While the index page serves for quick, quiet browsing, the detail pages preserve the original context, structural patterns, and design trade-offs.
